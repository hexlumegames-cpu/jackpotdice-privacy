About the Application

Jackpot Dice: Roll & Score is a free-to-play mobile puzzle and scoring game in which players roll five dice and attempt to achieve high-value combinations (such as three-of-a-kind, full house, straights, and Jackpot) to maximize their score. The game is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and as a native Android application distributed exclusively through the Google Play Store.

⚙️ Technical Details

The Application is built using web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) packaged via Capacitor for Android. All gameplay data, including scores and statistics, is stored locally on your device. No account registration or internet connection is required to play.

📢 Advertising

The App is supported by advertisements delivered via Google AdMob. An internet connection is required to load advertisements. Gameplay may continue while offline, but ads will not be served.
