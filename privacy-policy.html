This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Jackpot Dice: Roll & Score (package name: com.hexlumegames.jackpotdice), developed and published by HexLume Games. By downloading, installing, or playing the game, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

This policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and Google Play Store policies.

This policy applies exclusively to Jackpot Dice: Roll & Score and does not cover any third-party websites or services that may be linked from within the application.

Data We Do Not Collect

HexLume Games does not operate any backend servers for this game. We do not collect, process, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any other contact information
  • Your location, GPS coordinates, or IP address
  • Your device's contact list, camera, microphone, or photos
  • Login credentials or account information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Your gameplay data, scores, or statistics (these exist only on your device)
  • Any biometric data
✅
No user account is required. You can play Jackpot Dice anonymously with no sign-up of any kind.
3

Data Stored on Your Device

To provide a seamless gameplay experience, Jackpot Dice stores certain data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage mechanism within the Capacitor WebView. This data never leaves your device and is inaccessible to HexLume Games.

Data Key What It Stores Purpose
jd_best Your all-time highest score Display personal best on home screen
jd_stats Total games played, average score, jackpot count, bonus count, last 5 game scores Show statistics screen
jd_settings Vibration, confetti, notification badge, and sound effect preferences Remember your in-game settings
jd_daily Current daily challenge progress, completion status, and streak count Track daily challenges and streaks
jd_joker_count Number of earned extra-roll jokers Preserve reward between sessions
jd_paused_game Full game state at the moment of pausing Allow resuming an unfinished game
jd_lang Your chosen display language code (e.g., "en", "tr") Remember language preference

You can delete all of this data at any time by using the "Reset Statistics" option in the Settings menu, or by uninstalling the application.

📱
Permissions: Jackpot Dice requests only the minimum Android permissions necessary. The game uses the VIBRATE permission to provide haptic feedback during dice rolls (optional, can be disabled in settings), and the INTERNET permission solely to load advertisements. No other sensitive permissions are requested.

Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

Jackpot Dice is a free-to-play game supported by advertisements served through Google AdMob. The game displays three types of ads:

  • Banner ads — displayed at the bottom of the screen during gameplay
  • Interstitial ads — full-screen ads shown between game sessions
  • Rewarded video ads — optional ads you can choose to watch in exchange for an extra dice roll

To serve relevant advertisements, Google AdMob may automatically collect certain device information. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by HexLume Games. The types of data AdMob may collect include:

🆔 Advertising Identifiers

Google Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable, anonymous identifier used for ad personalization. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your Android settings under Google → Ads.

📡 Network Information

IP address (used for approximate geolocation for ad targeting), connection type, and device network status.

📲 Device Information

Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, language, and time zone — used to serve compatible ad formats.

📊 Ad Interaction Data

Whether an ad was shown, viewed, or clicked. This data is used by Google to measure ad performance and improve ad relevance.

⚙️
Opt Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time by opening your Android device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. Ads may still be displayed, but they will not be personalized to your interests.

Children's Privacy

Jackpot Dice: Roll & Score is intended for users aged 13 and older (or the minimum age required by applicable law in your country). The game is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the game is supported by Google AdMob, which may use the Advertising ID for ad personalization, we have not designated this app as a child-directed application under COPPA. Google Play's content rating for this application reflects this age restriction.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under the age of 13 has used the application, please contact us at hexlume.games@gmail.com. We encourage you to keep your Android operating system and the Jackpot Dice application updated to benefit from the latest security improvements.
